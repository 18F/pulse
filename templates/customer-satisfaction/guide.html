What domains are measured?

Pulse measures customer satisfaction survey use for publicly accessible .gov domains in the federal government's executive branch. Domains which do nothing but redirect to other websites are not measured.

How to participate

There are numerous survey tools for measuring customer satisfaction. Integrating one of them on your site should result in your site indicating that it has added a customer satisfaction survey.

Fields

  • Utilizes Customer Satisfaction Surveys
  • Values: No,Yes
  • Measurement is performed by detecting what URLs are queried while a page loads and then comparing them against a library of known customer satisfaction tools.
